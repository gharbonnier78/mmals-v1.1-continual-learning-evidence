# Claim Boundary

## Supported

- Robust five-seed SplitCIFAR10 external-bridge evidence over a frozen visual representation.
- Positive five-seed paired RC2O gains over validation-selected replay on SplitCIFAR100 and CORe50.
- A reproducible CORe50 failure diagnosis involving early-task collapse, old-context collapse and one-host collapse.
- A two-seed v2.3 partial rescue of class coverage, minimum-task accuracy, forgetting and host diversity.

## Not supported

- End-to-end continual acquisition of visual representations from raw pixels.
- State-of-the-art CIFAR or CORe50 performance.
- Standard CORe50 NC/NIC comparability for the custom 10 x 5 object stream.
- Positive backward transfer.
- Universal MMALS superiority.
- Successful oldest-context rescue in v2.3.
